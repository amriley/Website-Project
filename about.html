Baking is such a great way to de-stress and unwind whilst creating something that brings people together. One of my earliest memories is baking with my Nana, she would make cakes, flapjacks, welsh cakes, and so many other things with us and we were obviously always on hand to lick the bowl! With these recipes which I've put on this site you can share in some of the food which have been at the root of some of the greatest memories I have.
