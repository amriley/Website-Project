Some people think baking is a REALLY expensive hobby but it doesn't have to be. Below is a list of essential equipment that you'll need to get started. All the equipment on this list can be purchased relatively cheaply and the button will take you to a good place to get them. There are more expensive options out there but be warned, they aren't always better!

Electric Whisk

You CAN get away with a nice cheap handwhisk but I would ALWAYS recommend an electric. You can get them for under £20, they last for years & it'll save you a lot of arm ache!

Buy It Now
Mixing Bowl

An absolute ESSENTIAL! There is NO baking item which doesn't require at least one bowl! You can buy many individuals or grab a set! I'd say you need at least 2 & no more than 4.

Buy It Now!
Sieve

These literally cost pennies are SO useful in baking! So many baking ingredients work more efficiently in the mixture if sieved before hand; flour, icing sugar & coco-powder to name a few.

Buy It Now
Weighing Scales

It's hard to bake without scales as most (if not all) recipes give instructions in grams or millilitres. You can however get a good set for around £15 & they'll measure grams, millilitres & more!

Buy It Now!
Baking Trays

Baking trays can be confusing & you'll need to buy a couple of different types depending on what you want to make. Check out this guide & visit a local supermarket to get good quality, afforable trays.

Buy It Now
Cooling Trays

Cooling trays make sure that your bakes cool at the same rate on all sides. The bake's texture can be wrong if cooled without one & they'll cool faster, meaning you can eat them sooner!

Buy It Now!
Rolling Pin

You'll only need a rolling pin if you're wanting to make biscuits, or anything with pastry. They are vital for these things & only cost a small amount so, you may as well get one!

Buy It Now
Cookie Cutters

If you're going to make delicious mince pies or gingerbread at Christmas, these are a MUST HAVE. They come in so many shapes & sizes and can usually be found for less than £2 a set!

Buy It Now!
Measuring Spoons

Not strictly 100% necessary to bake (you can use your scales) but it does make life easier when recipes measure ingredients by tbsp & tsp! You can pick these up for under £5 so why not?!

Buy It Now
Spatulas & Spoons

There is NOTHING more important in baking than a good silicone spatula & a wooden spoon! They'll get all the batter out of the bowl and, more importantly, let you lick the bowl out!

Buy It Now!
